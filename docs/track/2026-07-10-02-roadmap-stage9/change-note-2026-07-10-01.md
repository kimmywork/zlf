# Change Note 2026-07-10-01: Split Deferred Stage 9 into a New Track

## Change

Close `docs/track/zlf-kernel-enhancements/` after required Stages 0–8 and move deferred Stage 9 plus the remaining relevant `docs/enhancement/roadmap.md` capabilities into this new parent track.

## Reason

Stage 9 combines several independent, high-risk domains: negation semantics, constraints, types, probabilistic proof evaluation, rule learning, WFS, memory management, and speculative runtime mechanisms. Keeping them in the completed kernel-foundation track obscures delivery state and encourages an unsafe monolithic implementation.

## Effect

- The predecessor track remains complete for Stages 0–8.
- This track starts in `proposed` state.
- S0/S1 discovery is next; optional modules require child approval.
- Existing deterministic positive tabling remains accurately scoped and is not renamed full SLG/WFS.
- Persistent virtual addresses and moving GC are evidence-gated rather than assumed.
