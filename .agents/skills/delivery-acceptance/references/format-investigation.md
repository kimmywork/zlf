# Format Fit: Investigation & Research

Use when reviewing investigation findings, research reports, or analysis. Check against these criteria in addition to Spec Fit.

## Methodology

- [ ] Research questions are clearly stated and scoped.
- [ ] Search strategy is documented (sources, keywords, time range).
- [ ] Inclusion/exclusion criteria for sources are defined.
- [ ] Methodological limitations are acknowledged.

## Evidence Quality

- [ ] Each finding is tagged with confidence level: confirmed / cross-referenced / inferred / asserted / from-agent-knowledge.
- [ ] Sources are diverse (multiple independent sources for critical claims).
- [ ] Source quality is assessed (authoritative, current, relevant).
- [ ] Gaps in evidence are explicitly noted.

## Traceability

- [ ] Every claim can be traced to a specific source or observation.
- [ ] Raw data and intermediate findings are preserved in `docs/track/<feature>/research/`.
- [ ] Conflicting evidence is reported, not hidden.
- [ ] "Gray zone" items (cannot verify) are excluded from conclusions or clearly marked.
